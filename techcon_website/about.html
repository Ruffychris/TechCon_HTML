History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 as a small local tech meetup and has grown into an internationally recognized technology conference attracting innovators, developers, and thought leaders from all over the world.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to foster collaboration, innovation, and knowledge-sharing across the tech industry. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of technology leaders and provide a platform for networking and learning.

Notable Past Speakers

Jane Smith

Jane Smith is a leading AI researcher who has delivered keynote speeches at TechCon 2018 and 2020, sharing insights on machine learning applications.

Michael Johnson

Michael Johnson, CTO of TechInnovate, inspired attendees at TechCon 2019 with his talk on emerging cloud technologies and digital transformation.

Sophia Lee

Sophia Lee, renowned cybersecurity expert, discussed the future of internet security at TechCon 2021, emphasizing best practices for safe innovation.
